The World's Greatest Heroes

United by a common purpose, the Avengers are Earth's mightiest heroes, standing against threats too great for any one hero to handle alone. From cosmic conquerors to rogue artificial intelligence, they fight to protect humanity, justice, and the future of our world. Through courage, sacrifice, and unwavering determination, they prove that true heroism knows no bounds.

And now, witness the legends, the warriors, the saviors of our time — here are our heroes, assembled and ready to defend the world!



Iron Man

Tony Stark, a genius billionaire and philanthropist. He built the Iron Man suit to protect the world from powerful threats. His intelligence and cutting-edge technology make him a key leader among the Avengers.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, super-soldier from World War II, Steve Rogers embodies leadership, courage, and unwavering morality. Armed with an indestructible shield, he fights to defend justice and freedom.

Thor

The mighty Norse god of thunder, Thor wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, granting him immense strength and the power to summon storms. He protects both Asgard and Earth from cosmic threats.

Hulk

Dr. Bruce Banner, a brilliant scientist who transforms into a raging green powerhouse when angered. Though struggling to control his strength, Hulk is a formidable force on the battlefield.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff, a highly skilled spy and assassin with a mysterious past. Natasha is an expert in combat, espionage, and tactics, making her an invaluable member of the Avengers.

Hawkeye

Clint Barton, a master archer with incredible accuracy and reflexes. Despite having no superpowers, his precision, strategy, and loyalty make him a crucial part of the team.

Black Panther

T’Challa, the noble king of Wakanda and the protector of his people, T’Challa possesses enhanced abilities and advanced technology. With his vibranium suit and sharp intellect, he upholds justice and tradition.

Scarlet Witch

Wanda Maximoff, a powerful sorceress with reality-bending abilities and unmatched energy manipulation. Wanda continuously hones her mystical powers while seeking to understand her true potential.

Doctor Strange

Dr. Stephen Strange, once a brilliant surgeon, he became the Sorcerer Supreme after mastering the mystical arts. He protects reality from supernatural and interdimensional threats.

Spider
Man

Peter Parker, a young hero with superhuman agility, strength, and the ability to cling to walls. Balancing heroism with everyday life, he uses his intellect and web-slinging skills to fight crime.

Ant
Man

Scott Lang, A former thief turned hero, Scott Lang uses the Ant-Man suit to shrink, grow, and command ants. Clever and quick-witted, he’s a devoted father who always finds a way to win against the odds.

War
Machine

James "Rhodey" Rhodes, an Air Force officer and Tony Stark’s trusted ally, Rhodey pilots the heavily armed War Machine suit. Disciplined and loyal, he’s a powerhouse on the battlefield and a dependable friend.

Star
Lord

Peter Quill, a cosmic outlaw and leader of the Guardians, Peter Quill wields twin blasters and a love for retro music. Cocky but caring, he fights with heart, humor, and a knack for trouble.

Captain
Marvel

Carol Danvers, a former pilot turned cosmic warrior, she possesses immense strength, flight, and energy powers. Fiercely independent, she defends the universe with unwavering determination.

Wong

Wong, The Sorcerer Supreme and Doctor Strange’s steadfast ally, Wong is a master of the mystic arts. Wise, disciplined, and occasionally sarcastic, he defends Earth from supernatural threats with unmatched skill.

Rocket

Rocket Racoon, a genetically enhanced raccoon with expert marksmanship and engineering skills, Rocket is as sharp-tongued as he is brilliant. Though tough and cynical, he deeply values his friends and will do anything for them.

Nebula

Nebula, a cybernetically enhanced warrior, she was raised by Thanos to be a ruthless assassin. Once consumed by vengeance, she found redemption and now fights fiercely to protect her newfound family.

Falcon

Sam Wilson, a former Air Force pararescue specialist. Falcon soars into battle with his winged jetpack. Loyal and courageous, he carries on Captain America’s legacy with honor and determination.

Vision

Vision, an advanced android with the Mind Stone, Vision possesses superhuman strength, flight, and wisdom. Gentle yet powerful, he seeks to understand humanity while protecting it.

Bucky

The Winter Soldier, once Captain America’s best friend, Bucky was turned into a deadly assassin. With a cybernetic arm and a haunted past, he fights to reclaim his identity and do what’s right.

Dora Milaje

Wakanda’s elite all-female warrior force, the Dora Milaje are led by the fierce and disciplined Okoye. Unmatched in combat and loyalty, they protect the throne and their nation with unwavering dedication.

Rescue

Tony Stark’s brilliant and capable partner, Pepper Potts is a leader in both business and battle. With resilience and heart, she steps up when the world needs her most.
